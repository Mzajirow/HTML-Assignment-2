About me

Hi, I’m Ejiroghene Okpirikhre, a content developer from Nigeria, now boldly transitioning into cloud computing.

My journey began with a deep desire to change my life professionally and personally. I knew I wanted more than just a job, I wanted a future-proof and secure career I could grow in. I began exploring new paths and ventured into software engineering, machine learning, data analytics, and several other programs in search of clarity. Along the way, I participated in the McKinsey Forward Program and the African Leadership Experience (ALX)’s Professional Foundation. It was during my time at ALX that I discovered cloud engineering and knew I had found my path.

Since then, I’ve earned two AWS certifications and dived deep into building scalable, cloud-based solutions. Every step I take is rooted in my values: resilience, integrity and a genuine commitment to solving problems and creating real value.

My AWS Certifications

Today, I’m building a career in tech with a clear focus on cloud computing. My goal isn’t just to succeed—it’s to be impactful, to help teams grow through innovation, and to become a trusted expert in my field. With dedication and hardwork, I aspire to work with a FAANG Company in the next ten years.

And when I’m not working or deep in studying about cloud engineering, you'll find me speaking somewhere or unwinding at the beach to reset and recharge.
