Why did I join the program and why AltSchool?

My short and simple answer is to learn and transition into a thriving career in Cloud Engineering.

Currently, I have a successful career in the media sector. I work as a content developer and client manager for one of Nigeria’s leading online media houses, a role I’ve held for almost five years. While the niche is relatively exciting and fun, I’ve been yearning for something more challenging, dynamic, and future-proof.

That hunger led me to tech, and I eventually decided to pursue a career in cloud engineering. I enrolled in a school and earned two AWS (Amazon Web Services) certifications—the AWS Cloud Practitioner and AWS Solutions Architect.

My AWS Badges

The badges look cool, right? However, with time, the excitement faded because I realized that certifications alone are not enough to break into tech and deliver real value to my future employers. I needed more. I needed to learn programming, scripting, and other cloud platforms beyond AWS, such as Google Cloud Platform (GCP) and Microsoft Azure. I tried self-learning through YouTube and online courses, which were great, but I knew I needed a more structured form of learning. That’s what led me to AltSchool. I’d heard about AltSchool on social media, specifically Twitter, so I decided to check the website. I reached out to a recent graduate of the program for a testimonial and he gave positive feedback. So here I am lol.
